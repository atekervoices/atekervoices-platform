About Ateker Voices

Ateker Voices is dedicated to preserving Ateker languages through community-driven voice collection.

We focus on Ateker languages: Ngakarimojong, Ateso, Soo, and Ik, spoken by Ateker people across northeastern Uganda and Kenya, to build robust speech recognition models that can understand and process these important cultural languages accurately.

All collected data is used to create language technologies that serve Ateker communities and help preserve their linguistic heritage for future generations.
